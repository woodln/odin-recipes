Fried Rice

Description

A classic Asian recipe that is delicious, quick, and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Cooked white rice
  • Eggs
  • Diced carrots
  • Chopped onions
  • Peas
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil

Steps

  1. Heat wok on high heat
  2. Add eggs and scramble, taking off heat and leaving aside once done
  3. Cook rice in wok, adding carrots, onions, and peas
  4. Add eggs, soy sauce, and sesame oil
  5. Beat quickly on high heat until thoroughly mixed

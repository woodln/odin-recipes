Lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Chicken broth
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Shredded parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F, grease baking dish
  2. Cook your ground beef in a pan
  3. Stir in marinara sauce and chicken broth

Layering lasagna

  1. Spread a third of marinara sauce into bottom of baking dish, top with 3 lasagna noodles
  2. Spread half of ricotta over pasta, top evenly with half of mozzarella, half of parmesan
  3. Top again with a third of the marinara sauce
  4. Add 3 more lasagna noodles, top them with last half of ricotta cheese
  5. Top once more with remaining mozzarella and parmesan
  6. Cover dish with foil and bake

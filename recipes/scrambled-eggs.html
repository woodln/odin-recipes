Scrambled Eggs

Description

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter or oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Crack three eggs into a bowl, add salt, and beat eggs until thoroughly whipped
  2. Heat pan on medium heat, add butter or oil of your choice
  3. Add 2/3rds of eggs and scramble them with heat-resistant spatula on low heat
  4. Once eggs are almost done, add remaining 1/3rd of eggs and beat again
  5. Once eggs are a bit less done than you'd like, take your pan off the heat and serve eggs
